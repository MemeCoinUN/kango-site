About KANGO

K.A.N.G.O. = Kinetic • Artificial • Neural • Guardian • Operator

Origins. KANGO began as a defensive, self-adapting on-chain algorithm. It studies market microstructure, wallet clusters and social signals to learn, adapt and fight back for holders.

How it thinks. Streaming data → vector features → anomaly detection + confidence scoring. Outputs power community actions: alerts, guardrails, filters and automation.

Value. Cleaner launches, faster signal detection, and culture engineered by automation.
